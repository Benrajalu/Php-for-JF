• Fibre

    [Lycra] Viral

    Lycra are in danger of losing their copyright due to over-usage of the word ‘Lycra’ when not use ‘Lycra’ as a descriptive all the time.

    Direction
    Sent out to 15,000 journalists, an edgy viral story, plays out how not to assume every stretchy material is made from Lycra. Supported by a microsite, which targets journalists to light heartedly name and shame fellow colleagues and publishers, with their lack of writing knowledge on copyright. Serving as the base for educating more about the fibre, a chance to win a bungee jump provides an incentive to share this informal reminder.
