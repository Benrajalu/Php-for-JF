• Winston VIP

    [JTI Winston] Experiential

    JTI Winston is the no.2 tobacco brand worldwide distributed in over 80 countries. Launching their new packaging at the Global Gathering and Freedom Festival to more than 50,000 people and top DJ’s from all over the world.

    Direction
    Creating an aspiration of being exclusive and on trend was the drive for this experiential and DM campaign. Branded red headsets were handed out during a flash mobbing and outdoor projections, kick started the need to attend the festiva and be part of the biggest music event.

    Branded VIP rooms and outdoor areas were created, to set the tone of being the select few and feeling valued. Winston member merchandise offered an incentive to register. Mixing deck bar-top displays and video footage would showcase the new packs being launched. Video pods and DJ booths allowed you capture and share the branded experience to others.
