    [Network Rail] TV Transvision

    Your my turn to cook station
    Edinburgh Station. Your best stop to shop
    Network Rail Station Shopping | Food & Drink

    Direction
    A six spot 20” TV transvision and digital backlit 96 sheet campaign to promote Network Rail’s mainline train stations, as a shopping destination, in their own right by rebranding station shopping in identifiable categories using subtle inner thoughts of commuters, which become station names. Impactful full imagery sets the tone, to break through the information filled mainland stations, across the UK from Euston and Farringdon to Manchester and Glasgow Central.
